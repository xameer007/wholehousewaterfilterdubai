Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Home Water Filters in Dubai

What is the average TDS level of Dubai tap water, and is it safe?+
Dubai's tap water TDS typically ranges between 150‚Äì450 mg/L. DEWA water meets WHO drinking water standards at the plant. However, by the time water travels through building tanks and pipes, TDS and contamination levels can rise. We recommend water below 100 mg/L for drinking, which our RO-stage systems achieve. See Dubai Municipality's published water quality guidelines for the full regulatory framework.
How much does a whole house water filter cost in Dubai?+
Our whole house water filter systems start at AED 1,999 for the Essential 3-stage system, AED 3,499 for the Pro 5-stage system (family villas), and from AED 5,999 for the Elite commercial-grade system. All prices include VAT and professional installation by Dubai-certified technicians. No hidden costs.
Can I install a whole house water filter in a rented Dubai apartment?+
Yes ‚Äî our systems use push-fit connections at the main water inlet that can be removed when you vacate, with no permanent modifications. We also provide landlord-approval documentation and handle all communications with building management if required. This is a very common request across Dubai's rental market.
How often do filter cartridges need replacing in Dubai's climate?+
Dubai's high TDS water and summer heat mean cartridges need more frequent replacement than in temperate countries. We recommend: sediment pre-filters every 3‚Äì4 months; carbon block filters every 6‚Äì8 months; RO membranes every 2 years; UV lamps annually. Our AMC (annual maintenance contract) handles all of this automatically.
Does a whole house water softener affect drinking water taste?+
Ion-exchange water softeners add a very small amount of sodium in exchange for calcium and magnesium. The taste difference is imperceptible to most people, and the sodium addition is far below WHO dietary limits. For those concerned, our Elite system includes a remineralisation stage ‚Äî which adds back beneficial minerals in balanced proportions.
What's the difference between a whole house filter and a point-of-use RO system?+
A point-of-use RO system only filters water at one tap ‚Äî usually your kitchen drinking water. A whole house water filter treats every litre entering your property, protecting your showers, laundry, dishwasher, and plumbing. Many Dubai families combine both: a whole house system for appliance and shower protection, plus a dedicated under-sink RO from PureFlow Dubai for drinking water purity.
Is your system approved by Dubai Municipality?+
Yes. All systems carry NSF/ANSI 42, 58, and 55 certifications and are approved by Dubai Municipality's Public Health Division. Our installation technicians hold Dubai Municipality plumbing certification. We provide all compliance documentation for building management or corporate procurement.
Which Dubai communities have the hardest water?+
Arabian Ranches, The Springs, and Mirdif ‚Äî supplied from older distribution networks ‚Äî tend to have higher hardness (300‚Äì500 ppm). Downtown Dubai, JBR, and Marina areas typically see 150‚Äì300 ppm. Our free water test measures your exact hardness level and TDS so you know whether a softener stage is genuinely necessary for your home.
